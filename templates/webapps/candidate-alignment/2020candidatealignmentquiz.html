Which Candidate Should You Support?

With a crowded Democratic field running for president, it can be tough to decide which candidate to support. This short political quiz is designed to make that process simple and help you answer the question of who to support.

This quiz will factor in your stances on the issues and what you like in a candidate (their identity, political experience, etc.). The goal is to match you with a campaign whose message and messenger will be most appealing to you.

These questions are based on our analysis of the 2020 candidate's websites and public statements in late August 2019 -- to see where each candidate stands on the wide range of issues but also what issues they did (or didn't) emphasize. This can show what a candidate's priorities would be, if elected.

This quiz should be only one factor in the process of making your decision of who to vote for. It is important to analyze candidates with your own scrutiny of a candidate's style, their past successes and/or failures, and other things that this quiz does not analyze.
