Democratic Primary Candidate Visualization

BUILT BY JOHN TUCKER
This web app depicts the relationship between between which presidential candidates Harvard College students support, the political issues students care most about, and which issues candidates spend the most time talking about. The stacked bar chart on the left displays the proportion of students that would rank a given candidate as their first choice. The comparative bar chart on the right compares the amount of time candidates spend talking about certain political issues on their social media with which political issues Harvard College students care about most.
